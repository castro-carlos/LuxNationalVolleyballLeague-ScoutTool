The main goal is to have a scout tool that helps identify possible strong points and vulnerabilities of a team playing in the Luxembourgish National Volleyball league.

The following steps act partly as a to-do list and also as guidance to reach the intended completed tool:
  1. Build a database using available data from the Luxembourg Volleyball Federation's website (https://flvb.lu/indoor/results/championship/men/division-nationale?saison134=5abf1462-614b-44ee-bb92-751f50becae8)
